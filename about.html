Meet the Chaos Creators

The masterminds behind the creative madness

Dominique

Creative Director & Chaos Coordinator

Dominique is the creative whirlwind who turns the mundane into something extraordinary. With a background in traditional art and digital design, they bridge the gap between the analog and digital worlds.

When not crafting visual stories that captivate and inspire, Dominique can be found exploring abandoned buildings for texture references or convincing strangers to participate in impromptu performance art.

Favorite tools: A trusty sketchbook, Adobe Creative Suite, and an unhealthy collection of vintage spoons repurposed as art tools.

"If your art doesn't occasionally scare people, you're probably doing it wrong."

Jose

Technical Director & Reality Bender

Jose is the technological wizard who makes impossible ideas possible. With expertise in programming, AR/VR development, and a knack for solving problems no one else can, they're the backbone of our most ambitious projects.

Their ability to translate creative concepts into functional digital experiences has earned them a reputation as the go-to person when someone says "Can we make it do this?" (The answer is almost always "yes").

When not coding or debugging, Jose spends time building unnecessarily complex Rube Goldberg machines and teaching their cat to interact with AR applications.

"Technology should feel like magic, but better because it actually works."
